Ramen Noodle

rāmen, [ɾameɴ] is a Japanese noodle dish. It includes Chinese-style wheat noodles (中華麺, chūkamen) served in a broth. Common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork (chāshū), nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 teaspoons granted ginger
  • 4 teaspoons granted garlic
  • 4 cups broth (i use chicken, but vegetable would work also)
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 ounce dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 packages instant ramen (noodle only!)
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions or chives
  • 2 chopped kale (or spinach)
  • 1 cups shredded carrorts (or any vegetable)
  • Sriracha to taste
  • crunchy golden panko crumbs for topping
